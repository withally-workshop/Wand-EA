# Wand EA & Chief of Staff — Operations Overview

A Wand-themed showcase site outlining the capabilities, operational model, and growth roadmap for an Executive Assistant transitioning into a Chief of Staff role.

## Live Site

**[https://wand-ea.netlify.app](https://wand-ea.netlify.app)**

> View the full role progression roadmap directly: [https://wand-ea.netlify.app/#roadmap](https://wand-ea.netlify.app/#roadmap)

## What This Site Covers

- **Role Overview** — A professional summary of the EA position at Wand, core competencies, and operational strengths
- **Calendar & Scheduling Expertise** — Systems and standards used to manage executive time across global teams and time zones
- **Chief of Staff Capabilities** — Strategic operational functions the role is structured to support and expand into
- **Administrative Support Services** — High-impact operational services including inbox management, travel logistics, meeting documentation, and stakeholder coordination
- **How It Works** — A step-by-step operational process from executive alignment through continuous improvement
- **Role Progression Roadmap** — A four-phase structured path from Operational Excellence to Chief of Staff impact
- **Executive Support Model** — The principles and practices that define how this role maximizes executive effectiveness

## Purpose

This site serves as a professional reference for stakeholders, hiring managers, or executive partners looking to understand the scope, structure, and strategic value of this EA function — and its readiness to scale into a Chief of Staff capacity.
